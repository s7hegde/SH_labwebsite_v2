# {% include icon.html icon="fa-solid fa-handshake" %}Expectations

__In the Hegde lab, we expect:__\
(1) Curiosity & Willingness to Learn\
(2) Good Organization & Proactive Planning\
(3) Open Communication & Transparency\
(4) Active solicitation of Feedback\
(5) Resilience & Self-care\
(6) Self-Assurance (in writing and grantsmanship)\
(7) Goal alignment and 'Mentoring up'

More details are available in the Official lab guide.
